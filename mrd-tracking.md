## Cross-Platform Comparison

The three assays complement each other:

| Assay | Type | Sensitivity | Best At |
|-------|------|-------------|---------|
| Signatera | Tumor-informed ctDNA | Highest specificity | Confirming true clearance |
| Northstar | Methylation-based | Moderate | Trend monitoring (not zero-able) |
| Personalis | Ultra-sensitive ctDNA | Highest sensitivity (PPM) | Detecting tiny residual signals |

Key insight: Signatera went to zero fastest and stayed there. Northstar never reaches zero (it detects background methylation). Personalis provided the most dynamic view of response.

---

## What MRD Enabled

1. **Confirmed recurrence** before imaging showed growth (Jun 2024)
2. **Measured treatment response** in real-time (weeks, not months)
3. **Validated parallel approach** — ctDNA dropped across all platforms simultaneously
4. **Guided therapy continuation** — ongoing mRNA vaccine justified by Northstar not being at floor
5. **Detected trace disease** at 0.01 MTM/mL (Signatera, May 2025) — below imaging threshold
